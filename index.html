More about insound.

Make more money

No BS, you keep 80% of every sale, and we take 20% to cover running costs (development, storage, servers, security, coffee, etc.)

Keep the rights

Take the power back! You will always keep 100% of the rights to your music if it's uploaded exclusively to insound.

Free to use

Uploading your music to insound will always be free... but you can also upload your music to streaming services for a small cost.

Manage your music

Create a profile, upload and manage your music online - quickly and easily. We also have a support team if you need any help.

Set your price

It's your music, which means you're in charge of how much you sell your music for on insound.
